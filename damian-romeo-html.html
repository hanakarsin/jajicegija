Damian Romeo - Age, Family, Bio

Publish date: 2024-03-10

Television and film actor who has gained popularity for his role as Matt Press in the Netflix series Ginny & Georgia. He appeared in 7 episodes of the first season of the popular TV show. He has also appeared in the series Impulse and The Expanse. 

Before Fame

He made his professional acting debut in the 2014 short film Magic.

Trivia

He portrayed the character Zeus in The Olympus Project. He has amassed over 10 different acting credits throughout his developing career. 

Family Life

He has two brothers. 

Associated With

He starred alongside Beverly Naya in the film Believe in You.
