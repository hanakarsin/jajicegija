Five Things To Know About Late Chinedu Bernard

Publish date: 2024-05-19

Nigerian actress, Chinedu Bernard slumped and died while cleaning a church in Enugu State on Friday.

Bernard was cleaning the Chapel of St. Leo the Great Catholic Church located inside the Federal Housing area in Enugu State when the incident happened.

The actress was rushed to the hospital by a priest identified as Rev Fr Uchendu Chukwuma after she slumped but was confirmed dead at the hospital.

Her remains have been deposited at the morgue.

The Nollywood actress before her death was featured in movies such as The Big Mama’s Stick, Money fever, The Mad, The Last Manhood, Mad Love etc.

Naija News has deemed it fit to share some important things to know about the actor.

1. The popular actress’s birthday is on the 26th of May.

2. Chinedu Bernard was given birth to in the family of Chief Bernard Obu Nwankwo.

3. The late actress is from Enugu State.

4. Chinedu Bernards Nwankwo is in her 20’s.

5. The actress before her death was in a relationship.
