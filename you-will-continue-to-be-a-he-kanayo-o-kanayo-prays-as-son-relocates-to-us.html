You Will Continue To Be A 'He'

Publish date: 2024-02-26

Veteran Nollywood actor, Kanayo O. Kanayo made a unique request for his son who is preparing to relocate to America for studies.

The actor via his Instgram page on Friday, 7th July, 2023 shared a video of his son who was admitted to a university in the United States of America, expressing pride while he offered a heartfelt prayer on his son’s behalf.

As part of his prayer, the actor specifically requested that his son maintains his identity and orientation as a ‘He’, despite societal pressures on this new journey.

Kanayo also included some other important aspects in his prayer, such as focusing on academic achievements rather than tattoos, and urging his son to uphold the values instilled in him during his upbringing.

In his words: “America will be proud of you. Your achievements will cause America to celebrate you. You will imbibe the good of the land and eat the good therefrom. . You will continue to be a HE from Ezinihitte Mbaise in Nigeria. Your focus shall be on your books and job NOT ON TATTOS. May you never depart from your upbringing. DO NOT FORGET GOD. You shall ever remain on God’s RADAR AND GPS. Good luck, boy.”
