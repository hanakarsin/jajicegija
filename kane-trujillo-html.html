Kane Trujillo - Age, Family, Bio

Publish date: 2024-07-21

Social media star who has gained popularity for the text-in-video comedies he posts to his kane TikTok account. His videos have earned him over 3.2 million followers on the platform. He has also boxed competitively and has posted boxing related posts for his 190,000 followers on his kanetrujillo Instagram page. 

Before Fame

He played basketball for his high school team. 

Trivia

One of his most popular TikToks is a comedy skit about how the Justin Bieber song "Intentions" was recorded. In December of 2021, he posted a photo alongside comedian David Spade on his Instagram. 

Family Life

He has a brother named Kody. His parents have appeared on his kanetrujillo Instagram account. 

Associated With
