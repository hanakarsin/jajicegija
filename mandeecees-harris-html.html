Mendeecees Harris - Age, Family, Bio

Publish date: 2024-04-12

Reality star and entrepreneur best known for his appearances on Love & Hip Hop: New York. He worked as a music manager and was also the owner of Beat Factory studio in the Bronx before being sentenced to eight years in prison for drug trafficking in 2016.  

Before Fame

He was involved in the real estate industry before his music began. 

Trivia

He was imprisoned for federal drug trafficking before being released on bail. 

Family Life

He married Yandy Smith in 2015 and the couple has two children together, Omere and Skylar. He also has two sons from previous relationships, including Mendeecees Harris Jr. 

Associated With

He starred on Love & Hip Hop: New York along with Tahiry Jose.
