Tatiana Shmayluk - Age, Family, Bio

Publish date: 2024-02-02

Ukranian rock singer who is best known as a member of the metalcore band Jinjer. As the lead vocalist for the band, she has participated in the creation of albums such as 2014's Cloud Factory and 2016's King of Everything.

Before Fame

She first joined the band Jinjer in 2010, though the band was officially formed a year earlier, in 2009. 

Trivia

Her band has been signed by Napalm Records. She has gained over 460,000 followers on her personal tati_booyakah Instagram account. 

Family Life

Her mother's name is Vera, and she posted a photo with her to her Instagram account in May 2018. 

Associated With

her band has toured with the death metal band Arch Enemy and their guitarist Michael Amott.
