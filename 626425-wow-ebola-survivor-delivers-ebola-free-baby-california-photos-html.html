Ebola Survivor Dr Ada Igono Delivers Baby (PHOTOS)

Publish date: 2024-04-10

One of Nigeria’s great heroes who fought with their lives during the outbreak of Ebola virus in Nigeria, Dr. Ada Igonoh delivers her baby girl few hours ago in California.

Dr. Ada ever since her pregnancy has been undergoing series of medical surveillance until the birth of her baby at Great El-Monte Community Hospital, California.

In order to celebrate both mother and child, the staff and friends of First Consultants Medical Centre are jubilant as they welcome a new lease of life into the institution but could not also forget the loss of their colleague during this battle.
