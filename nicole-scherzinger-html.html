Nicole Scherzinger - Age, Family, Bio

Publish date: 2024-02-22

Singer and actress who gained fame as a member of The Pussycat Dolls, best known for their hits "Don't Cha" and "Stick Wit U." She won Dancing with the Stars in 2010. In 2019, she became a judge on FOX's The Masked Singer. She has had small roles in several major films, including Men in Black 3 and Moana. 

Before Fame

She studied musical theater at Wright State University and played a role in Show Boat. She was also a member of short-lived girl group Eden's Crush. 

Trivia

In 2011, she released the solo album, Killer Love, which featured the hits "Right There," and "Don't Hold Your Breath."

Family Life

She is the daughter of Alfonso Valiente and Rosemary Elikolani. She has a younger half-sister, Keala. In 2016, she started dating tennis player Grigor Dimitrov. She was previously in a long-term relationship with Formula 1 racer Lewis Hamilton. She is currently in a relationship with rugby player Thom Evans. They got engaged in June 2023. 

Associated With

She performed with Kherington Payne in The Pussycat Dolls.
