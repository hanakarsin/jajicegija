It is believed that real friends are the ones who come in and stay for years.

This, former Tahidi High actor Joseph Kinuthia, alias Omosh, understands perfectly well.

Omosh on Thursday, March 25, took a journey down the memory lane by sharing a frame of himself with one of his fellow casts on Tahidi High, Mr Kilunda.

While celebrating the popular Throwback Thursday, the star shared the old photo of themselves as he reminded his fans how harsh Teacher Kilunda was.

''#TBT with the strict Teacher Kilunda,'' Omosh captioned the photo.
