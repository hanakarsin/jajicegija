Dariya Stavrovich - Age, Family, Bio

Publish date: 2024-06-26

Lead vocalist of the Russian alternative rock band the SLOT. Her nickname is Nookie and she recorded her first solo album titled Zhivy in 2013.

Before Fame

She studied at Nizhni Novgorod College of Music and at the Vocal Faculty Institute of Modern Arts where she specialized in pop and jazz singing.

Trivia

She was invited to provide vocals for The SLOT thanks to the acquaintance of Igor Lobanov with Max Samosvat, the latter an ex-vocalist of the band Epidemia.

Family Life

She was born in Velsk, Archangelsk Region, Russia.

Associated With

She and Alla Pugacheva were both Russian born vocalists.
