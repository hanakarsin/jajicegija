Yusuf Panseri - Age, Family, Bio

Publish date: 2024-08-19

Curly-haired lip sync and point-of-view content creator who rose to fame by sharing videos on his panseriyusuf TikTok account. His relatability led to him garnering more than 10 million fans. He has posted multiple videos with fellow content creator Simone Berlini. 

Before Fame

One of his earliest lip sync performances was set to Bad Bunny's "NI BIEN NI MAL."

Trivia

He promoted the refurbished iPhone company Swappie in a 2019 Instagram post. One of his popular TikTok videos showed him using a toy gun to distribute lollipops. 

Family Life

He was born in Italy. His mom is Moroccan and his dad is Italian.

Associated With

He is a member of the dance and lip sync TikTok collective defhouse alongside Alessia Lanza and Simone Berlini.
