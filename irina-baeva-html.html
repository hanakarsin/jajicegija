Irina Baeva - Age, Family, Bio

Publish date: 2024-07-09

Best known for her work throughout the Mexican telenovela industry. In 2015, she was cast to star in the telenovela Pasión y Poder as Daniela Montenegro. In 2022, she landed a starring role in the TV series Amor dividido. 

Before Fame

She studied acting at the Centro de Educación Artística in Mexico City, Mexico. She took classes at the Televisa studios, and began her schooling there in 2012.

Trivia

She debuted in 2015 in the telenovela Muchacha Italiana Viene a Casarse. She has also amassed a sizable following on Instagram with over 3.5 million followers.

Family Life

She was born and raised in Moscow, Russia. She's been in a relationship with telenovela actor Gabriel Soto. 

Associated With

On February 12, 2016, she posted a photo to her Instagram account of her alongside Michelle Renaud. The two have been fellow cast members on the Mexican telenovela Pasión y Poder.
