30-year-old lady who got married at 17 gives marital advice

Publish date: 2024-03-13

A woman who has been married for thirteen years has taken to social media to give marital advice to young wives and women who are about to walk down the aisle and say I do to the man of their dreams.

The woman identified simply as Mphephethwa who revealed that she was married at the age of 17, noted that she has been married to her husband for thirteen years and she her three beautiful kids.

According to the 30-year-old woman, the tips on marriage that she shared on Twitter is to help young wives and wives to be survive marriage.

She advised women to try and start their businesses early, adding that it is expensive to take care of children both financially and emotionally.

The woman further noted that communication is key in a marriage. She asked women not to assume anything but talk about everything with their partners.

Mphephethwa stated that women should have the patience to manage things maturely because they would be hurt by their husbands in a way that no one would understand.

She advised that young wives should have the heart to forgive and not hold grudges. She added that in-laws should be respected no matter how friendly they are.
