Actor Ninalowo Knocks AGN President, Emeka Rollas Over BBN Comment

Publish date: 2024-02-08

Nollywood actor, Bolanle Ninalowo has slammed the national president of the Actors Guild of Nigeria’s, Emeka Rollas for declaring BBN evictees unfit for Nollywood. 

Naija News understands that veteran actor and AGN president, Emeka Rollas in a recent Instagram post tagged multiple blogs, whilst declaring participants of the Big Brother Naija show unfit for Nigeria’s film industry.

He wrote; “Nollywood! Not a dumping ground for evictees!!! Case closed,” 

Reacting to the comment, the father of two via his Instagram tackled Emeka Rollas, revealing he’s wrong for making such a statement.  

He also questioned the veteran actor requiring who the industry is meant for since it’s not a dumping ground for evictees.

He wrote; ‘This one hit me hard! Oga @emekarollas you’re wrong for this one baba!! Abeg who is Nollywood for? Maka needs to be enlightened.’

Recall four housemates were evicted from the Big Brother Naija reality TV show on Sunday, 5th September in the ongoing season 6 of the show tagged ‘shine ya eye.”

The four housemates evicted were Tega, Boma, Michael, and Peace during the eviction night anchored by the host of the show, Ebuka Obi-Uchendu.

Naija News reports it will be the first time that four housemates are evicted from the show at once.

Tega was the first housemate to be evicted on Sunday due to the low number of votes from fans during the ‘kingsize eviction’ as tagged by the organizers of the show.

Micheal and Peace were to follow before Boma also got kicked out of the show.
