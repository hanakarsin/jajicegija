Connor Darlington - Age, Family, Bio

Publish date: 2024-05-16

Web video star best known for posting comedy videos to TikTok, often dressing up as characters for his videos. He has over 5 million fans on the platform. His username is connor. He also posts cover songs and prank videos to his Connor Music YouTube channel. 

Before Fame

He first began posting videos to YouTube in late 2012. 

Trivia

One of his most popular YouTube videos, "PRANKING my CRUSH with SONG LYRICS GONE SEXUAL?!," has earned over 1 million views. He also received over 187 thousand hearts on a comedic TikTok of him building a cat tree for his uninterested cat. 

Family Life

He is from England. He began dating Liana Jade Brooker, with whom he launched the collaborative YouTube channel Connor And Liana. They were married in March 2020. The couple announced in December 2021 that they are expecting their first child, a son, together.  

Associated With

He posted a cover of the Justin Bieber song "Sorry" in October 2015.
