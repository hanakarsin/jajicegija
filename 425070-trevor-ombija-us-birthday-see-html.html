Trevor Ombija Off to US Ahead of His Birthday: &quot;My People in Atlanta Georgia, See You Soon&quot;

Publish date: 2024-02-01
  • Citizen TV journalist Trevor Ombija will be turning a year older on Friday, September 10
  • Trevor has taken a tour to the USA to celebrate his 33rd birthday
  • Trevor announced his departure on the evening of Sunday, September 5, via his Instagram account

Celebrated Citizen TV news anchor Trevor Ombija has flown to the United States to celebrate his birthday.

Trevor, whose birthday will be on Friday, September 10, shared a photo of himself on a flight that took off on Sunday, September 5.

"My people in New York, Seattle, Atlanta Georgia, New Jersey standby. I only need a source of heat because am coming over with dagga and unga floor. See you all soon," Trevor wrote

The Monday Report host who recently disclosed his breakup with his 10-year girlfriend will be turning 33 years old.

Trevor will hook up with his close friend, Larry Madowo.
