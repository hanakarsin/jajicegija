Randa Nelson - Age, Family, Bio

Publish date: 2024-03-04

Singer-songwriter, actor and YouTuber, along with her twin sister Nina. She and Nina have earned over 550,000 subscribers to their channel NinaAndRanda. 

Before Fame

She was featured in Justin Beiber's "U Smile" music video, and studied at a Russian ballet conservatory. 

Trivia

She is one minute older than her twin sister and has danced with the Miami City Ballet, American Ballet Theater and the Joffrey. She grew up with Kendall and Kylie Jenner and did a short film with them when they were 10.

Family Life

Her whole family loves to go to the island of Kauai at least once a year. She and her entire family are vegan. Her parents run the YouTube channel VegSource. In addition to Nina, she has a younger brother.

Associated With

She and Nina became friends with fellow YouTube twins Monica and Shelby Church.
