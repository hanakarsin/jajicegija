Eniko Parrish - Age, Family, Bio

Publish date: 2024-08-17

Gained fame through her relationship with comedian Kevin Hart. The two married in 2016. She is extremely popular on Instagram with over 3.3 million followers on her enikohart account. 

Before Fame

She and Kevin began dating in 2011, and they got engaged in 2014.

Trivia

She often posts yoga pictures and videos and other workout content to her Instagram account. 

Family Life

In 2017, she and Kevin welcomed a son named Kenzo. They welcomed their second child together, a daughter named Kaori Mai Hart, in September 2020. Kevin has two children from a previous marriage. 

Associated With

Torrei Hart, Kevin's ex-wife, has stated that she is on good terms with Kevin and Eniko.
