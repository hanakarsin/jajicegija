Nobert Young hosts surprise birthday for actress wife Gloria Young at 50

Publish date: 2024-02-25

Nollywood actress Gloria Young turning 50 was an interesting event as her colleagues and friends trooped out to celebrate her with a football match.

Gloria's actor husband Nobert Young put together a novelty football match between the female celebrities, tagged Gloria Young Ladies FC, and the males - Nobert Young Men FC.

The match ended 2-1 with the ladies thrashing the men. Nobert served as the goalkeeper for the men's team.
