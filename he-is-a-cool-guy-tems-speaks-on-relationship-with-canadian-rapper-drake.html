'He Is A Cool Guy'

Publish date: 2024-03-01

Nigerian singer, Temilade Openiyi, popularly known as Tems, has opened up about the rumoured relationship between her and Canadian rapper, Drake.

Naija News reports that this comes after Tems and Drake were spotted having lunch with his team at an undisclosed location and many fans began speculating that something was brewing between them.

However, in a recent interview with Beat FM, Tems was asked if Drake had made a pass at her when they met.

Responding, the singer said Drake did not woo her nor attempted to have anything more than friendship.

According to her, the Canadian singer is widely known for his friendly character and people who are close to him will attest that he is genuine.

Tems noted that Drake is a cool guy, however, she sees every male around her as a brother, because of her kind of personality.

When the presenter teased her not to ‘brother zone’ everyone so she could know the right partner, the ‘Crazy things’ crooner said she will have a feeling of it whenever she meets the right guy.

In her words: “Oh no, errmm he did not, he is very cool I think, I think you know what Drake is known for I don’t know but I think if you come to him like you guys talk, he’s a genuine type, I think he is a cool guy and also the kind of person I am, I just like to chill, I egbon everybody. It is not that I want to, it is just that is how I see everyone.

“I’m not sure I have met anyone and I felt like you are not my brother, everyone is my brother. I know that that guy will someday fall from heaven or something and I will be like wow, I will feel it, I won’t say, Drake is my brother, he is my guy.”
