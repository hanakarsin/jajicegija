1. Tyndale University

Tyndale University, located in Toronto, is a Christian institution that provides diverse programs at the undergraduate, seminary, and graduate levels. It delivers both undergraduate and postgraduate degrees and diplomas to its students.

Tyndale University offers flexibility in completing courses with options like full-time, part-time, modular programs, night classes, online training, and more. Tyndale University is one of the universities in Canada without application fees for those coming from USA and Canada.

2. University of Alberta

The University of Alberta is among the cheapest university in Canada. It boasts a significant reputation and popularity, hosting a diverse student body from various parts of the world.

The university stands out with extensive academic offerings, featuring 500 graduate programs, 250 specializations, and 300 research programs.

Prospective international and local students are encouraged to make their application process well in advance. Notably, the university does not impose application fees for undergraduate degrees.

3. Royal Roads University

Situated in Colwood, British Columbia, Royal Roads University is a public institution providing diverse academic programs.

The university offers seven undergraduate and three graduate programs in management, tourism, hospitality, law, business, environmental sciences, and communications.

Students can pursue these programs in person, online, or through a hybrid approach. Royal Roads University does not charge any application fees for all students, making the application process more inclusive and accessible to a broader range of individuals.

4. Carleton University

When looking for cheap universities in Canada, you can consider Carleton University. Recognized as both a research and teaching institution, Carleton University provides a diverse array of courses.

To qualify for admission to Carleton University, prospective students must demonstrate proof of completing three years of high school or university in any country where English is the primary language.

Carleton University stands out as one of the most cost-effective options for international students seeking to study in Canada without needing the IELTS exam. There is no fee to apply to Carleton.

5. Redeemer University

Redeemer University is a private Christian liberal arts and science university in Ancaster, Ontario, Canada. It was established in 1982 by the Association for Reformed Political Action (ARPA) and the Reformed Christian community.

The university is affiliated with the Christian Reformed Church in North America. Redeemer University offers a range of undergraduate programs in arts, science, business, and education. The university emphasizes a liberal arts and sciences education with a Christian perspective.

6. University of Victoria

The University of Victoria (UVic) is a public research university in Victoria, British Columbia, Canada. UVic offers undergraduate and graduate programs in various fields, including humanities, sciences, engineering, social sciences, business, and fine arts. The university is known for its research contributions in several areas. Its one of the popular Canadian universities without application fees for international students.

7. Quest University

Situated in Squamish, British Columbia, Canada, Quest University is a non-profit institution specializing in liberal arts and sciences. Quest University waives application fees for international and local students applying to graduate and undergraduate programs. The university provides a relatively accessible admission process compared to other institutions.

8. University of Prince Edward Island

The University of Prince Edward Island (UPEI) is a public university in Charlottetown, Prince Edward Island, Canada. UPEI was established in 1969 by merging St. Dunstan's University and Prince of Wales College. The university has since grown and evolved, offering diverse academic programs.
