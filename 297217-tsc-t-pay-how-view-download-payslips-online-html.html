TSC T-Pay: How to view and download your payslips online

Publish date: 2024-06-08

Members of the teaching profession have a lot to thank the Teachers Service Commission (TSC) for. With the inception of the TSC portal, it is no longer necessary for teachers to physically show up at offices to get some things done. For instance, the TSC T-Pay feature allows teachers to view and download their payslips online without any hassle.

TSC was established in 1967, but its history dates back to the 1950s when teachers led by retired President Daniel Moi vigorously fought for the formation of one teacher body that would manage the affairs of all teachers in the country. Over the years, the commission has greatly grown in size and operational capacity as it boasts of more than 750,000 members.

What is T-Pay?

T-Pay is an online service provided by TSC that allows teachers registered under the commission to access their online payslips. To enjoy the services offered on the portal, you only require the Teachers Service Commission number and password that you will use to log in to the system. Also, note that the service is offered free of charge.

How to register on T-Pay

To access your online payslip, you need to complete the registration process to be issued the TSC number. Initially, the process was conducted online. However, teachers need to visit the local TSC county office and present their national ID and other required documents.

How to view your TSC payslip

You do not need to queue at the local TSC offices to access your payslip in the current digital age. You can easily log into the new TSC T-Pay portal to view or download your payslips. Here is a simple procedure you should follow to access your payslips.

  • Open the TSC T-Pay site on your browser.
  • Scroll to locate the TSC payslip login window
  • You will be prompted to enter your TSC number or ID number and password in the boxes provided.
  • Click on the login tab once you are done.
  • If you have forgotten your password, click on the 'reset password' to generate a new password.
  • Once you have successfully logged in, you should choose the Latest Payslip option from the list of items on the left side of your screen.
  • The latest payslip will be displayed on the screen. If you want a hard copy, you should click on the Print tab. The payslips for four months are displayed.
  • If you wish to download the payslips of previous months, you should choose the 'View Payslips' sub-menu on the left of your screen and specify the year and month.

It is imperative to note that the T-Pay feature allows you to send your latest payslip to other parties such as banks and saccos to facilitate the acquisition of a loan. You can view all your third party transactions on the portal.

With this comprehensive guide on TSC T-Pay, you can easily view and download your payslips online from the comfort of your home or office without any hassle. The good news is that the process is easy, and it can be done on any suitable device as long as it can access the internet. Moreover, you have the freedom to choose the payslip for a specific month or year.
