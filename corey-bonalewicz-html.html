Corey Bonalewicz - Age, Family, Bio

Publish date: 2024-07-03

Radio personality and web star who goes by the name Corey B. He's known for his work as a DJ for Kidd Nation. He became an on air personality for the Chicago radio station B96. He shares food and fitness content on his iamcoreyb Instagram account. His Corey B YouTube channel features comedic vlogs that often include his girlfriend. 

Before Fame

He was working for B 97 in New Orleans when he was selected as a Kidd Nation DJ.

Trivia

He has more than 1.9 million followers on Facebook, more than 750,000 followers on Instagram and more than 120,000 subscribers on his YouTube channel, where his most-viewed video is of a shark throwing up on him.

Family Life

Born in Tampa, Florida, he later moved to New Orleans, Louisiana.

Associated With

He posted a picture to Instagram with Max Schneider after the actor stopped by his radio station.
