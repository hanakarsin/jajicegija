Diego Klattenhoff - Age, Family, Bio

Publish date: 2024-07-29

Canadian actor known best for his roles as Mike Faber on Homeland and Donald Ressler on NBC's The Blacklist. He has also had guest spots on 24, Psych, and Supernatural.

Before Fame

His early television credits include episodes of Doc and Wild Card. At the start of his acting career, he supported himself by working as a bartender.

Trivia

He played the role of Shane Oman in the popular 2004 movie Mean Girls.

Family Life

A native of French River, Nova Scotia, he settled in Toronto, Ontario in his late teens. He later relocated to Los Angeles, California with his wife and son.

Associated With

He played the recurring role of hockey player Ivan in Anne Heche's ABC television series, Men in Trees.
