Boss Moves: Socialite Zodwa Wabantu to Launch Funeral Service Business

- At this point, she has proven that whatever she puts her mind to, she can achieve

- The dancer who hustles without stopping has decided to spread her entrepreneurial wings once again

- Zodwa has announced that she will be starting a funeral service business complete with body storage facilities

Zodwa Wabantu is diversifying her streams of income. The socialite works as a dancer, chicken farmer, entrepreneur and now a funeral service provider.
