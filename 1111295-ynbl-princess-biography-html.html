YNBL Princess: Who is she? What role does she play in the YNBL Nation? Only the hottest facts about the YNBL Princess biography are here for you.

YNBL nation

So, YBNL Nation (Yahoo Boy No Laptop Nation) is an autonomous record label, which was founded by famous rapper Olamide, in 2012.

YBNL is a well-proclaimed record label which has gotten quite popular and reputable within the last six years. This record label members have steadily increased, and now it has more than three artists and two producers. The CEO of YBNL (Olamide), was the first artist under this record label. And he happens to be one of the most successful local rappers and one of the biggest artists in Nigeria.

YNBL princess biography

Now let's talk about the only princess among these gentlemen. So, Temmie Ovwasa is a songwriter, singer, spoken word artiste, guitarist, and poet, who has released two singles on YBNL’s roaster Afefe and Jabole.

She was born and bred in Ilorin, Kwara State. Temmie Ovwasa became the first female artist to be signed to Olamide’s record label YNBL Nation, and in April 2016 she was referred to as the YBNL Princess. And for right now, she plans to stay the YBNL’s princess.

Interestingly enough, she is a final year student of Ladoke Akintola University of Technology where she is studying Medical Anatomy. Beautiful, talented and smart!

Temmie is a self-taught guitarist, talking drum player, and keyboardist. She had never visited Lagos State before Olamide invited her to join the YNBL Nation. After she accepted this great opportunity, she became the YBNL Princess. In the mean time, she has been trying to ‘spice up’ her normal tomboyish style.
