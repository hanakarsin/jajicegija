Matt Lintz - Age, Family, Bio

Publish date: 2024-02-13

Young actor with an already impressive resume by the time he had become a teenager which includes credits in Kill the Messenger, Pixels, Piranha 3DD and The Crazies. He was also featured in Lifetime's Army Wives.

Before Fame

He began his acting career when he was four years old as a commercial and voice over actor. His first film role was in Rob Zombie's Halloween 2.

Trivia

He has television credits in Sleepy Hollow and Revolution. He starred alongside Lucy Hale in A Cinderella Story: Once Upon a Song.

Family Life

He comes from an acting family as one of four children born to actress Kelly Collins Lintz. His siblings are Madison, Macsen and Mackenzie.

Associated With

He was a guest-star alongside Jason Lee in Memphis Beat.
