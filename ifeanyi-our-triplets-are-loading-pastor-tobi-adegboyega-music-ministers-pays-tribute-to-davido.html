Ifeanyi: 'Our Triplets Are Loading'

Publish date: 2024-04-06

Celebrity Barman, Pascal Okechukwu, popularly known as Cubana Chiefpriest, has shared the moment Pastor Tobi Adegboyega encouraged Davido and Chioma to continue to stay strong.

He expressed optimism that the Nigerian singer, and his fiancee, would be having triplets as a replacement for their son, Ifeanyi.

Naija News recalls Davido and his fiancee, Chioma Rowland lost their three-year-old son to a drowning accident on Monday, 31st October 2022.

However, Cubana Chiefpriest took to his Instagram page to share moments from the Sunday service of Celebrity pastor, Tobi Adegboyega.

In the video, the clergyman and his music ministers honoured Davido with a remix of his song titled ‘Stand Strong’.

The song is no doubt a means to console the lovebirds who are going through hard times following the death of their son.

The celebrity Barman noted that God will fix everything about Davido and Chioma because He is their only backup.

He affirmed that triplets are sure and God will make it happen.

In his words: “Our steps are guided by Jehovah @davido @thechefchi God must fix us. God is our only backup. We stand strong with you guys and we are coming out of this together. Our triplets are sure.
Loading %… So help us God amen”

Davido Speaks On AWAY Festival

Meanwhile, Davido has postponed his scheduled AWAY music festival to a new date.

Naija News reports that the singer made this known through his management in a press statement shared online.

The statement noted that postponing the event was a difficult decision but it was necessary and all ticket buyers will be honored on the new date.

Davido‘s management also apologized for disappointing his fans and promised to come back with a bigger and better show on Saturday, November 18, 2023.
