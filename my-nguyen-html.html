My Nguyen - Age, Family, Bio

Publish date: 2024-04-26

Chef whose food photos and cooking videos have made her wildly popular on Instagram. She is the author of the blog My Healthy Dish, which aims to provide simple and health-conscious food recipes to its readers. She created an Instagram account specifically tied to her blog.

Before Fame

She majored in communication and media studies at California State University - East Bay.

Trivia

She published the book My Healthy Dish: More Than 60 Fresh and Easy Recipes for the Whole Family in April 2016. She has gained over 654 million views on YouTube.

Family Life

She has been married and has twin daughters.

Associated With

She posted an Instagram photo with multiple-time NBA All-Star Klay Thompson in September 2016.
