Charlie Berens - Age, Family, Bio

Publish date: 2024-07-08

Comedian and journalist who's earned 2.2 million YouTube subscribers thanks to his sketches, interviews and a popular segment called the Manitowoc Minute. He's been featured on CBS, FOX, TBS, and Funny or Die. One of his most popular sketches features a parody of Midwest dialect. It has gained over 3 million views on YouTube. 

Before Fame

He launched his career after joining the MTV News series Choose or Lose. He earned degrees in journalism and geography from the University of Wisconsin. 

Trivia

He won a Regional Emmy Award while working as a reporter for the television station KDAF. He almost did not release his first episode of the Manitowoc Minute, due to his uncertainty over how it would be received. While working in journalism, he steadily built his voice and style as a comedian by developing stand-up material and occasionally posting funny videos on the internet.

Family Life

He shared an extended family photo on Instagram on Christmas Day 2019. He is the second eldest of 12 children.

Associated With

He collaborated with actor Kevin Hart on the series Co-Anchor Confidential.
