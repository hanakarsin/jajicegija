The 50-year-old has topped Kenya trends on Twitter ever since her confirmation on the morning of Tuesday, January 12, which was announced by Cabinet Secretary Najib Balala.

Going by the reactions on Twitter, it appears many believe that there were people better placed to get the role with names such as Lupita Nyongo and Victor Wanyama who are more popular than the model, popping up.

Naomi and Balala meeting

The supermodel's new gig was confirmed following a weekend meeting with Cabinet Secretary Najib Balala.

During the meeting, she expressed her admiration for the improvement in the infrastructure particularly the upgrading of Malindi Airport to international standards thus boosting tourism.

Naomi who is a regular visitor to the Kenyan coast used the opportunity to invite international golfers to visit and enjoy some of the world-class facilities in the region.

The beauty queen spent the 2020 December holidaying in Malindi where she reportedly owns some businesses.

She is said to have been gifted two houses at the coastal town by her ex-lover where she unwinds occasionally.
