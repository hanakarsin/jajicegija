Bensoul biography

Are you wondering about Bensoul real name or birthplace? Or you are interested in learning about Bensoul tribe? The artist’s real name is Benson Mutua Muia, and he was born on March 4, 1996. This means that Bensoul age is 24 years old as of 2020—quite an excellent age for the current stature of his career.

The multitalented musician created great interest in music at a very tender age while growing up in Embu town. Bensoul used to pick musical instruments whenever he got the chance, teaching himself to play some of them.

As a child, Bensoul’s father played the guitar while his mother sang in the choir. This influence made the talented singer create more interest in music. He started learning the guitar when in Class Six, a skill that he has perfected today.

The artist attended high school, whereby he continued to improve his skills. One of Bensoul’s high school teachers commended him for his fantastic voice. The teacher mentioned that Bensoul sang with so much soul in his voice. And that is how he ended up combining his name Benson with soul.

Bensoul has an exciting history with musical instruments that go way back. While he was young, the multitalented musician created immense interest in music as well as playing musical instruments.

His father and mother, both skilled in music massively influenced him at a tender age. This even made Bensoul start guitar lessons while in Class six. Bensoul enjoyed watching people playing the instrument and wanted that experience. Since a trainer was costly for the family to afford, he would learn to play the chord the entire week and take advantage of church instruments for further practice.

How many of Bensoul songs do you know? Probably many. And he did start somewhere, writing his first song when still a young man. According to the artist, he composed his first song while in high school, which was inspired by Sauti Sol’s “Lazizi.”

3. He quit Civil Engineering at the University of Nairobi

Bensoul is a classic example of following one's passion. In 2013, while studying Civil Engineering at the University of Nairobi, he met H_art The Band that helped him polish his music writing skills.

The band even requested Bensoul to write music for them. That is when he realized that he was way better as a musician than a Civil Engineer and chose to terminate his course. He stopped studying for the profession after three years, and his mother was very supportive of the decision.

4. He is signed to Sol Generation Records

Since Bensoul songs are amazing, Sol Generation Records saw great potential in the artist making it big in the industry. According to Savara, one of the members of Sauti Sol music group and pioneers of Sol Generation Records, Bensoul is a rare breed. He is immensely talented as an artist, holding skills that others lack.

Bensoul announced his debut at Sol Generation Records on February 2019. And fans couldn't wait to see what was in store after the signing and beginning of a new chapter in his career.

5. Bensoul is a multi-instrumentalist

The talented musician can play different musical instruments amazingly well. He perfected this craft while in high school, where he was regularly playing different instruments, focusing on keys. Eventually, he became the school's pianist. Bensoul can also play double bass and violin and is even interested in playing the saxophone.

6. A personal experience inspired his first song, Lucy

Who doesn’t know Bensoul’s great hit, Lucy? It was a great song that is heavily listened to even today. The inspiration for the song came from a girl that he loved but had many suitors. In the song, he tells the girl that she is not worth all the struggle.

7. Bensoul wrote Nikuchukie for his father

The song Nikuchukie is a tale of how Bensoul was raised by his mother alone and in poverty. His father had the capability of uplifting them be he didn't. This inspired him to write the song. However, Bensoul doesn’t hold any grudge against his father.

8. He is a fantastic lyricist

Other than Bensoul songs, the musician and creative lyricist has written songs for other well-established artists. According to Bien of Sauti Sol, Bensoul is a great writer, one of the best that he has ever encountered. He has composed for artists like Alikiba, Nyashinski, Benpol, H_art the Band and Mercy Masika and many others.
