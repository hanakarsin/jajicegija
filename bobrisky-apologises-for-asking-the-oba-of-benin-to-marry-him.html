Bobrisky Apologises For Asking The Oba Of Benin To Marry Him

Publish date: 2024-03-06

Controversial Nigerian cross dresser, Idris Okuneye popularly known as Bobrisky has tendered a public apology after he was slammed for asking the Oba of Benin to marry him.

The crossdresser who is currently in Benin, attending an event by Ehi Ogbebor showed his excitement at being in the city.

He said: ‘’The queen is here. We are here to disturb Benin. Oba of Benin, Bob don enter o. Come and marry me Oba. ‘’

Some Edo people did not take Bobrisky comment lightly, threatening to attack him if they see him.

In a new video posted on his Instagram page, the cross dresser apologized to the Edo people for his statement and said he was simply joking around.
