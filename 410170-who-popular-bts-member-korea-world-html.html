Who is the most popular BTS member in Korea and around the world

Publish date: 2024-06-11

BTS is a South-Korean boy band whose name was inspired by the Korean expression Bangtan Sonyeondan, which translates as Bulletproof Boy Scouts. The group has been in existence since 2010 but debuted in 2013. They boast a worldwide fan base who are referred to as BTS armies. Netizens have been curious to find out who is the most popular BTS member.

BTS is globally acclaimed, and fans love their graceful choreography and energy on stage. In 2017, BTS rebranded to ''Beyond The Scene,'' which represents youth who do not settle for their present reality but instead go forward to achieve growth. This has enabled them to connect with audiences outside their home country. So, who is the most popular BTS member?

Most popular BTS member in Japan

Netizens have been asking, ''Who is the most popular BTS member in Japan?'' Nehan, a popular K-Pop idol website in Japan, recently released a list of Japan's most popular male idols.

Surprisingly, BTS members took the top three positions. V tops the list as the most popular K-Pop idol, followed by Jungkook at number two, while Jimin came in third. The three make up the 'Maknae' line, which constitutes the youngest members of the band.

Most popular BTS member in Philippines

Phillippines boasts the highest number of BTS fans, constituting 21% of the total BTS fan base. Most of them are concentrated in the country's capital, Manila. In the Phillippines, the BTS army has warmed the hearts of many through various campaigns they conducted, including donating free rice and raising $40,000 for flood victims.

Suga, one of the shyest band members, directly complemented the Filipino fans, calling them passionate and pleasant. This comment was widely celebrated, mainly because the singer hardly offers public compliments. The rapper's fans were delighted, and several of them sent back their love to the idol through online platforms.

Most popular BTS member in China

V, who has sometimes been referred to as the visual representation of K-Pop, is the most loved BTS member in China. He is the band member with the most considerable following on Weibo, China's biggest social media platform.
