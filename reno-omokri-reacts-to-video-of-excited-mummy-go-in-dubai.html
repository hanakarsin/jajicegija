Reno Omokri Reacts To Video Of Excited Mummy GO In Dubai

Publish date: 2024-06-25

Former presidential aide, Reno Omokri has taken to social media to react to a video of Funmilayo Adebayo,popularly known as Mummy GO excitedly having a good time in Dubai. 

Naija News understands that the founder of Rapture Proclaimer Evangelical Church of God was recently spotted in a video having a nice time in Dubai, United Arab Emirates.

The controversial preacher who caught the attention of social media users through her hell fire messages recorded herself in her pyjamas in a luxurious hotel in Dubai.

The preacher in the video shared online took out time to reveal the luxury room she lodged in and the gadgets in the room, also taking her followers and fans through her mini kitchen as she reveals her face through a mirror.

Reacting to the video, Reno Omokri via the microblogging platform, Twitter noted that Mummy GO’s excitement over her Dubai trip proves that religion strives because of suffering.

He maintained that when one travels out, things they fast and pray for will seem so elementary. He further urged the controversial evangelist to enjoy her stay and try as much as she can to avoid hell.

”That Mommy GO video in Dubai where she was excited like a teenager vindicates what i said in 2021. Religion thrives in Nigeria because of suffering. If you relocate abroad, the things you fast and pray for now seem so elementary. Enjoy Dubai Funmi, but avoid hell.”He wrote
