Tulisa Biography, Net Worth, Cousin Dappy and Ndubz Split

Publish date: 2024-02-28

Tulisa is a British singer/songwriter who is a member of the Camo Town, London-based tri-hip-hop group N-Dubz, alongside his cousin Costadinos Contostavlos, nicknamed Dappy and his friend Richard Rawson (“Fazer” ). The group received widespread critical acclaim and scored eight Top 40 hits on the singles charts before going on hiatus in 2011 to focus on their individual solo careers and never regroup.

What happened to Talisa after splitting from N-Dubz and how she coped in her solo career with some of the questions people have about the British musician Here’s everything you need to know about the sensation of song.

Brief biography

Tulisa was born in Camden Town, London on July 18, 1988 to a mother who was part of the 1980s band – Jeep alongside three of her sisters. Just five years old, her mother was divided under the Mental Health Act because she suffered from bipolar and schizoaffective disorder. She was then under the care of her uncle – Byron Contostavlos. At the age of fourteen, he enrolled her in the Quintin Kynaston Schoolat St John’s Wood, she however took her GCSE exams at Haverstock Secondary School, Haverstock, London.

Cousin – Dappy and Ndubz Split

Tulisa joined N-Dubz in 2000. The band was formed by her cousins ​​Dappy and Fazer, who were already rapping together before deciding they wanted a female vocal in the band. So they implicated Tulisa. The group was then called Lickle Rinsers Crew before switching to N-Dubz and they began performing in their home town of Camden; soon their fame spread even beyond the city.

“You Better Not Waste My Time” was their first single to be released and was made available for download until they dropped their self-released single “I Swear” in 2006. The single earned them their first notice for the general public. In 2007, N Dubz dropped another single ‘Feva Las Vegas’ which charted on the UK charts, peaking at 57.

In May 2008, they dropped their fourth single “Ow” along with a music video that garnered over 4 million views on YouTube within just a month of its release. In November of the same year, the group releases its first album Uncle b under the records of the whole world. The album was dedicated to Dappy’s father, Byron Contostavlos, who managed the band but died shortly before signing to their new label – All Around the World. The album charted on the UK Albums Chart with number eleven.

Their second album Against All Odds was released in November 2009 and also reached the UK Albums Chart at number 6. In 2010 they signed with US label Record Jam and released their third studio album. Love.Live.Life under the label. The album became their third platinum album.

In 2011 the band left the Def Jam label and went on hiatus to focus on their individual solo careers and never reunited. Tulisa took on a judging role on the eighth edition of The X Factor, while her cousin Dappy released her solo single ‘No Regrets’.

As a member of the group, Tulisa has won five MOBO.Awards, one BRIT Award nomination, two Urban Music Award nominations, two platinum-certified albums, one gold-certified album, three silver-certified singles, and more.

After the band broke up, Talisa moved on with her life and career. As mentioned above, she was on the judges for the eighth and ninth series of The X Factor . She led the Groups category and her winning act Little Mix became the most successful girl group in X Factor history. The following year, she dropped her debut single ‘Young’, which went to number one on the UK Singles Chart.

She subsequently dropped two more singles ‘Live It Up’ and ‘Sight Of You’ which both charted in the UK top twenty. In November of the same year, she released her first solo album “The Female Boss”, which charted on the UK Albums Chart at number 35. Tulisa also dabbled in acting. in 2011 she starred in two British films; a horror movie Demons Never Die and a comedy Gangster Big Fat Gypsy.

Net value

The English singer/songwriter currently has an estimated net worth of $4 million; which are earnings from her hard work as a singer, actress and television personality.

Other Facts About Tulisa

In 2012, a six-minute sex tape featuring the singer and her former lover, Justin Edwards, hit the internet. Tulisa, however, obtained a legal injunction preventing the tape from circulating. She took legal action against Edwards for releasing the tape; its manager, Chris Herbert, was suspended as a consultant on X-Factor.

Tulisa is a practicing Catholic although she was baptized into her father’s Greek Orthodox religion.

In 2013, she was arrested on suspicion of supplying class A drugs, she denied the allegation and the case was later dismissed, with the judge having reason to believe that prosecution witness Mazher Mahmood, had lied to the court.
